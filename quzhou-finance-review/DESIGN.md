## Style Prompt

Dark financial documentary with a restrained institutional research tone. The frame feels like an analyst briefing room at night: deep navy atmosphere, charcoal panels, muted gold labels, controlled green/red market movement, subtle grain, soft vignettes, and deliberate motion. All visuals are synthetic infographics, trend lines, icons, and typographic data. No real K-line screenshots and no external market data.

## Colors

- Background navy: `#07101b`
- Charcoal panel: `#111820`
- Deep gray: `#20252c`
- Muted gold: `#c9a24d`
- Up green: `#42b56b`
- Down red: `#d94a3f`
- Text white: `#f1f4f8`
- Secondary text: `#aeb7c2`

## Typography

- Primary Chinese font: `Noto Sans SC`, `Source Han Sans SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif
- Numeric font: `DIN Alternate`, `Arial Narrow`, `Noto Sans SC`, sans-serif

## Motion Rules

- Title: fade in with slight camera push.
- Price nodes: numeric count-up / pulse animation.
- Rising segments: green line draws upward.
- Falling segments: red line draws downward.
- State-owned capital scene: shield glow.
- Semiconductor scene: chip lights up and rocket lifts.
- Terminated acquisition scene: announcement file appears and red arrow drops.
- Five causes: sequential pop-in.
- Summary: price axis highlights node by node.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use real candlestick screenshots or real quote feeds.
- Do not use cyberpunk, neon-heavy, flashy tech, cartoon, or marketing-hype styling.
- Do not change the locked price data.
- Do not add unsourced financial claims beyond the storyboard narration.
